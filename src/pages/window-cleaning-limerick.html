Our window cleaning service offers flexible scheduling options for our clients in Limerick. Choose from 6-week or 12-week cleaning intervals which allow you to enjoy sparkling clean windows all year round.

Pure water fed pole systems clean windows by pumping deionized or reverse osmosis water (with minerals removed, measuring 0-5 TDS) through an extendable pole to a brush head that can reach heights of 30 feet from the ground. We scrub the window with the brush while pure water flows through it, agitating and lifting dirt, then rinse thoroughly from top to bottom. Because the water contains no minerals, it dries completely spot-free without squeegees or towels, typically within 15-30 minutes. This method is safer than ladders, uses no chemicals, and is particularly efficient for high or hard-to-reach windows.

  • Pure water window cleaning works by using deionized or reverse osmosis (RO) filtered water that has had all minerals and impurities removed. This water, being chemically imbalanced, attracts dirt, grime, and other impurities from the window surface as it is applied using a water-fed pole system. Once the windows are scrubbed, the pure water rinses away the dirt, leaving the glass spotless without the need for drying. Because there are no dissolved minerals in the pure water, it evaporates without leaving streaks or spots, resulting in a pristine, streak-free finish.

  • Pure water window cleaning can be used effectively in the rain because rainwater is naturally soft, containing fewer minerals compared to hard tap water. Since pure water has no impurities, it cleans the windows by absorbing dirt and grime, leaving a streak-free finish regardless of the weather. Even if rainwater lands on the cleaned windows, it won’t leave spots because pure water has already removed the dirt and contaminants. As a result, cleaning windows with pure water in the rain still provides excellent results, as the rain itself won’t undo the cleaning process.
