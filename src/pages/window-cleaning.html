Our Process for Cleaning Windows

At Cleanway we employ an efficient and effective process for cleaning windows using a water fed pole system. As the purified water flows through the pole and onto the windows, it effectively lifts and carries away the dirt and debris. This thorough cleaning process leaves your windows sparkling clean, both inside and out.

The use of purified water also eliminates the need for chemical detergents, making it an environmentally friendly option. We will bring extension ladders and all the equipment needed for the job, so you won’t have to lift a finger. Our technicians take extra care to make sure they don’t harm your landscaping, siding and flowerbeds.
